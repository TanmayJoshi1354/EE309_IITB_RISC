This folder contains all design related documents of the designed IITB-RISC microprocessor.

All states in the device are enlisted in states.pdf, and the files pertaining to the instructions LM and SM have their own states which are not included in states.pdf but are present in LM_HFC.pdf and SM_HFC.pdf

All instruction Hardware flowcharts are present in FINAL_Hardware_flowcharts.pdf

All instruction state diagrams are present in Instruction_state_diagrams.pdf

Every control word related detail is provided in control_design.pdf and control_word.pdf has all state-wise control word values.

Datapath.pdf has the final datapath implemented.

ALU.pdf and Sign_extender.pdf are examples of design documents for some components.

Approach.pdf has a holistic view of what was to be accomplished.
